QuicConnection

QuicConnection class

Adapter implementing libp2p.TransportConn around a quic_lib Libp2pQuicConnection.

This is a foundation-level implementation. The QUIC handshake and stream multiplexing are handled internally by quic_lib; this adapter exposes the connection lifecycle and metadata required by the libp2p transport interface.
